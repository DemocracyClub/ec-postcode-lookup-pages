A recall petition is the process by which an MP can lose their seat in the House of Commons.

By signing a recall petition, you are saying you want your MP to lose their seat. You only need to sign the petition if you want to see your MP removed.

You have six weeks to sign the petition at your signing place.

You’ll need to take photo ID with you. Check the list of accepted forms of photo ID.

Registered postal voters will be sent a signing sheet by post.
